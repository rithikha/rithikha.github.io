BAMBOO PAVILION

This 3D modelling project was created using Rhino and the Grasshopper plug-in for parametric workflows. The organic shape utilizes the properties of bamboo and the principles of area minimization to create a strong, yet lightweight structure. Inspired by Camboo by Luca Poian Forms.

BAMBOO   >>
High compressive strength, low weight, fire resistant, and elastic. Bamboo is a fast-growing plant and a renewable resource with versatile, multi-purpose usage.

ENNEPER SURFACE   >>
First introduced by Alfred Enneper in 1864, an enneper surface minimizes total surface area within a given constraint. Minimal surfaces are abundant in nature and physical processes such as the formation of soap film, butterfly wing cells and leaf structure. These surfaces have high application potential in building design as they maximize structural efficiency and optimize material distribution.
