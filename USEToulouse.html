LIFELINES OF THE CITY

Created using Mapbox GL JS, this web map visualizes the public transportation networks of Toulouse, the fourth-largest city in France. Social and physical mobility are linked in many ways and as cities continue to increase in size, the accessibility and quality of a their transportation infrastructure will be crucial in helping create more equitable, healthy urban environments as well as happier communities. This map was designed to highlight the major forms of public transport available in Toulouse such as metro lines, trams, and bus routes, along with population density data from the 2016 census
